Pick an athlete to start

Charts show athletes' stats as percentiles within their position group: for example, 80th percentile for bench press means that an athlete's bench press is higher than 80% of athletes in their position. The number above each bar is the real stat. Hover over each bar for more information.

    Add new athlete

    To add a new athlete to the database, enter their Name, Position, Year, and Number to the right. Input anthropometric, strength, power, agility, and speed stats as well as any notes below. The minimum fields needed to create a profile are: Name, Position, and Year. Click the "Save Athlete" button once you are done. You can use the "Clear Stats" button or refresh the page to start over.


    Edit existing athlete

    To edit the bio or stats of an existing athlete, use the button below to pull up their existing information in the form. Note: if you change the athlete's Name or Year, you will create a new profile separate from the old one. Once you are satisfied with your changes, click "Save Athlete".

      About this tool

      This tool is designed to build, visualize, and save reports on athletes' physical attributes and off-field performance metrics. Athletes are benchmarked against five years of data from their position group, and the charts provide a visual diagnostic of their overall performance.
