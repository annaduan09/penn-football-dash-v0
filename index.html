      Add new athlete

      To add a new athlete to the database, enter his Name, Position, Year, and Number to the right. The minimum fields needed to create a profile are: Name, Position, and Year. Click the "Save Athlete" button once you are done. You can use the "Clear Stats" button or refresh the page to start over.


      Edit saved athlete

      To edit a saved athlete, click "Saved Athletes" to pull up his profile. Note: if you change the athlete's Name or Year, you will create a new profile. Once you are satisfied with your changes, click "Save Athlete".

          Overview

          This tool is designed to visualize athletes' physical attributes and off-field performance metrics. Athletes are benchmarked against five years of data from their position group, and the charts provide a visual diagnostic of how their testing scores compare to their group.


          Usage

          Add Data

          This page serves two purposes: users can edit information in existing athlete reports, searchable using the "Saved Athletes" button. Users can also add new athletes to the database. The way the app distinguishes between changes to existing profiles and new profiles is by whether the Name-Position combo in the form currently exists.


          Athlete Report

          This page is for viewing reports from saved athletes. Use the "Saved Athletes" button to select the athlete of interest. The charts will visualize all of his available stats as percentiles within his position group. Hover over individual bars to reveal exact percentiles and position-group median values.
